Adobo

Sweet Chicken Adobo with eggs and potatoes!

A Philippine dish of fish or meat usually marinated in a sauce containing vinegar and garlic, browned in fat, and simmered in the marinade.

Ingredients:

  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Ground Black pepper
  • Garlic
  • Red Onions
  • Bay Leaves
  • Egg
  • Cooking Oil
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, garlic, vinegar, water, pepper and bay leaves in a pot. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Strain chicken and set aside.
  2. Sauté chicken and garlic in oil until golden brown.
  3. Pour cooking liquid and simmer until sauce is thick and coating the chicken. Transfer on a serving plate and don't forget to add boiled eggs then serve.

Tortang Talong Recipe

Description

Tortang talong is a Filipino eggplant omelette that delights the palate with its simplicity and rich flavors. Grilled eggplants are flattened, dipped in beaten eggs, and then fried to golden perfection. The resulting dish boast a smoky undertone from the charred eggplant, complemented by the savory blend of eggs. Often seasoned with salt and pepper, and occasionally enhanced with minced garlic or onions, tortang talong is a satisfying and versatile dish, served as a side, main course, or even as a filling for sandwiches.

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant:1 piece chinese eggplant
  • Egg:1 piece
  • Salt:1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Black Pepper:1/8 teaspoon grounded
  • Oil:1/4 cup cooking oil
